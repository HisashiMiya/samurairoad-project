One-line definition
FeatureWorks is not a set of convenience features. It is a system that places “inescapable moments” where you feel reality again — with AI walking beside you, not leading you.
日本語（外向け一文）:
FeatureWorksとは、AIと共に便利になるための機能ではなく、
現実を感じ直してしまう瞬間を“逃げ道なく”配置する設計群である。

Features at a Glance

external summary

① Silent Walk (Core)

silence control

AI intentionally stays quiet. No explanation. No visible “progress.” Sometimes nothing happens — and that’s the point.

  • Designed to be remembered, not “liked.”
  • Triggers are rare and non-repeatable in the same way.
  • Meaning arrives late — or never.

② Resonance (Body Hack)

haptics

A short, irregular vibration appears without telling you why. Your body notices first; your mind tries to catch up later.

  • No constant effects. Only as a betrayal of silence.
  • Built for “maybe it was nothing” ambiguity.

③ Stealth Walk (Entertainment Layer)

game-mode

A quiet walking challenge: fewer screen checks, fewer sudden speed changes, less modern noise. No rankings — only personal “stillness.”

  • Onboarding hook that leads to the core.
  • Rewards unlock deeper silence, not louder content.

④ Memory Fragments (Addiction)

incomplete collection

You collect imperfect fragments: noisy audio, blurred images, broken lines. Completion is impossible by design.

  • No 100%. A missing piece always remains.
  • Each revisit shifts the fragment slightly.

⑤ Patron Stela (Sponsors)

honor + responsibility

GitHub Sponsors becomes participation in “editing memory.” A patron name marks a spot — but what’s released is ambiguous, not a clean story.

  • Public honor on the surface.
  • Private responsibility underneath.

What we will never do

  • Shortest-route navigation
  • Full map unlock / 100% completion meters
  • Rankings & distance competitions
  • “AI is amazing” explanations
  • Explicit fear-mongering
